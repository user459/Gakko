Lawrence High School

Welcome To Lawrence High

At Lawrence High, children are taught to discover and nurture their innate capabilities. Academics and co-curricular activities are given equal importance.Established by the Cauvery Education Trust, Lawrence High focuses on relevant education to help children grow up into responsible and excelling adults who can face the challenges of the ever evolving modern work environment. We began in Koramangala over 27 years ago and continue to expand our premises to accomodate more children and provide space for extra curricular activities. Lawrence follows the ICSE curriculum which is recognised world over.



Campus Life

Laboratories

Indoor & Outdoor Play Areas

Academics

Sports

Sports is very important to us. It is an integral part of our educational methodology. 6 Physical Training teachers impart physical education to students and teach football, cricket, hockey, and more.

Art & Craft

Art stimulates imagination and learning arts is necessary for the physical, emotional, intellectual and social growth of an individual. From Kindergarten, we teach painting, drawing and work on their origami skills as well.

Music Classes

We have three amazing music masters who teach songs, keyboards, guitar, and drums. (Our Band ‘Bawse’ came ‘Second” in Mumbai in the All India Music Competition 2018)

Karate & Taekwondo

Self defence is an important aspect to learn in today’s world. At Lawrence, martial art classes are a must. This is a good way to keep children fit and confident as well. The school takes part in various Taekwondo competitions and the children win many medals from time to time.

Facility

  • Computer Labs

    Our school has 2 fully equipped computer labs. Our computer studies curriculum is designed keeping in mind the practicality of computer application in today’s world. We prepare students to become fluent with learning software of any kind. At Lawrence, your child will be future-ready when it comes to working with systems and applications.

  • Large Library

    Lawrence believes that a reading child is a thinking child and a thinking child is a happy child. The library at Lawrence was developed with this belief in mind. Principal

Mrs Sujatha Nagraj

Mrs Sujatha Nagraj

“Empowerment of every child at all levels by meeting the intellectual, spiritual and emotional needs of the child is what we strive for.” Mrs. Sujatha Nagraj has over 17 years of experience in the field of education she holds a Masters in Mathematics & Education. As a passionate and caring teacher, she strongly believes that education is a dynamic, natural, progressive and systematic change that occurs in a human being.
